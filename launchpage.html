TECH CHECK

TEENS IN TECH

A youth organization for boys of color, also known as the Hidden Genius Project(HGP) provides African American males the knowledge and skill set, to develop mobile and also web based applications.

WOMEN IN TECH

Black girls code is devoted to showing the world that black girls, can code and do so much more, such as programming robots and also creating websites! Less than 5% of technology is made up of women. Let's change that!

EVOLVING TECH

AS the years go by, technology flies right over our heads, literally. From un maned drones, to cars that drive themselves, what will we do when technology over runs us? It's up to us to fix that. The youth has a voice too!

Requirements

Creativity

Jobs that are being created by people, are mainly ideas that are thought of "outside the box." So, no matter what technological field you endeavor, have a strong imagination.

Higher education degree

Today, education is key. Most of the time, if you only complete high school, 6 figure salary paying jobs won't invest their time into you. So, by attending college and mainly majoring in Computer Science, or something of that nature, you will get paid well.

Experience

Many tech jobs today require certain skills. Yes, if you go to college and graduate and majored in computer science, you can get a job, but a lot of these jobs want people who really know what their doing. So, by starting early, you are opening yourself up to larger doors.

Self directed

Not only do tech related jobs require self motivation, but anyone you work for require this. When a problem gets hard don't give up, keep working.

Organization

When working countless hours, and thousands of lines of code, organizing files and projects on your computer is very important.
